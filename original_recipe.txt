Ingredients:

- 10 ounces pancetta diced
- 16 ounces spaghetti
- 4 large egg yolks
- 2 cups parmesan cheese grated
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 2 cups cooked chicken breast
- 1 tablespoon italian parsley

Directions:

Step 1: Gather all ingredients.
Step 2: Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
Step 3: Coat a 9x13-inch baking dish with nonstick cooking spray.
Step 4: Cook pancetta in a medium skillet over medium-high heat until lightly browned and crisp, about 5 minutes.
Step 5: Set aside.
Step 6: Boil spaghetti in lightly salted water for 5 minutes.
Step 7: Drain pasta and reserve 2 cups pasta water.
Step 8: Meanwhile, add egg yolks toÂ a large mixing bowl and whisk until smooth.
Step 9: Gradually pour 1 1/2 cups hot pasta water over egg yolks, whisking vigorously, making sure to whisk constantly so eggs do not scramble.
Step 10: Whisk in Parmesan cheese, then heavy cream.
Step 11: Add cooked pasta, pancetta, and chicken to the sauce, toss to combine.
Step 12: Transfer pasta mixture to prepared dish.
Step 13: Bake until hot and sauce has thickened, 20 minutes, stirring halfway through.
Step 14: Remove from heat, stir and let stand for 2 to 3 minutes allowing sauce to continue to thicken.
Step 15: Additional pasta water can be added, as desired, for a saucier pasta.
Step 16: Garnish with parsley and extra Parmesan cheese.

Tools:
whisk, bowl, dish, skillet

Methods:
heat, cook, crisp, boil, brown, bake
